# Glossary

- **LISA** - Longitudinell integrationsdatabas for sjukforsakrings- och
  arbetsmarknadsstudier. SCB's longitudinal database integrating sickness
  insurance and labour market studies.

- **IoT** - Inkomst- och taxeringsregistret. The income and taxation register
  at SCB.

- **FAD** - Foretagenas och arbetsstellenas dynamik. The firm and workplace
  dynamics register at SCB.

- **BAS** - Befolkningens andringar. The population change register at SCB.

- **NUTS-3** - Nomenclature of Territorial Units for Statistics, level 3,
  corresponding to Swedish lan (counties).

- **SUN 2020** - Svensk utbildningsnomenklatur 2020. The Swedish education
  classification.

- **MONA** - SCB's Microdata Online Access platform.

- **SAFE** - SCB's Secure Access For Enterprises remote-access platform.

- **jobbskatteavdrag** - The Swedish earned-income tax credit.

- **famansforetag** - Closely held company subject to the Swedish 3:12
  dividend rules.

- **3:12 rules** - Special tax rules for closely held firms in Sweden,
  separating labour and capital income for owner-employees.

- **CRPS** - Continuous Ranked Probability Score. A proper scoring rule for
  probabilistic forecasts.

- **Pinball loss** - A quantile-regression loss; also known as quantile loss.

- **Mondrian conformal** - Stratified split conformal prediction with
  per-stratum calibration.

- **FAIR4RS** - FAIR principles applied to Research Software (Findable,
  Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable).

- **OSOR** - Open Source Observatory. The European Commission's open-source
  catalogue.

- **EUPL-1.2** - European Union Public Licence version 1.2. A copyleft licence
  with compatibility provisions for compatible licences enumerated in its
  appendix.

- **DEP5** - Debian copyright format version 1.0, used by REUSE 3.0 as a bulk
  licence-declaration mechanism.

- **SCB** - Statistiska centralbyran. Statistics Sweden.
